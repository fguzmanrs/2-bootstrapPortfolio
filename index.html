About Me

Hello!

Welcome, clearly you are making some great life decisions if you are visiting this page. What's my best life decision? Entering the world of coding. I recently found myself at a crossroad; I decided to take a chance and now I am learning fullstack coding at UCR's Extension Bootcamp.

I will use the fullstack skills I'm learning now to work as a front-end developer. I find myself coming into the field with experience in teaching, social work, and literature that provides my projects with an important sociocultural viewpoint in our increasingly interconnected world. There is a need for universal design that caters to a wider range of audiences as the world becomes flat. One area I feel particularly passionate about and have experience in is reflecting an agency's mission by expanding on its accessibility and inclusivity initiatives.

I value a solid life-work balance. Taking time to live outside work and explore a wide set of experiences fosters my creativity, feeds my curiosity, and allows me to continuously challenge myself. Some of my favorite activities are thus traveling abroad, exploring art, reading, and learning new languages. It's this Joie de Vivre that I bring to my work.
